## STEP 9: WAIT FOR REVIEW (3-6 Months)

### Timeline:

- **December 1**: Submission deadline
- **December-January**: NSF administrative review (completeness check)
- **January-March**: Peer review by experts
- **March-April**: NSF program officer decision
- **April-June**: Award notification

### Review Process:

1. **Administrative Review** (1-2 weeks)
   - NSF checks if application is complete
   - If missing something, you'll be contacted
   - Respond within 48 hours if requested

2. **Panel Review** (2-3 months)
   - 3-5 expert reviewers read your proposal
   - Evaluate: Intellectual Merit, Broader Impacts, Commercial Potential
   - Rate and write comments

3. **Program Officer Decision** (1-2 months)
   - Reviews panel recommendations
   - Makes final funding decision
   - May request revisions or additional info

### Possible Outcomes:

1. **Funded** (20-30% acceptance rate)
   - You get $275K for 6 months
   - Start work immediately
   - Eligible for Phase II ($750K for 2 years)

2. **Declined**
   - Reviewer comments provided
   - Can resubmit improved version
   - Most successful projects get funded on 2nd or 3rd try

3. **Revision Requested**
   - NSF wants to fund but needs changes
   - You have 30 days to respond
   - High chance of funding after revision
