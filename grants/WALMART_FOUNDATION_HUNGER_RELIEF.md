## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Fast Food Rescue Network tackles food insecurity in Clark County by rescuing surplus food from restaurants and delivering it to homeless shelters and sober living centers. With Walmart Foundation support, we will:

- **Feed 100,000+ people** in Clark County's food deserts
- **Rescue 60,000 lbs of food** that would otherwise be wasted
- **Create 2 jobs** for individuals in recovery
- **Save restaurants $75K-150K** in tax write-offs

## PROJECT DESCRIPTION

**The Problem**:
- 150,000 Clark County residents are food insecure
- Meanwhile, restaurants throw out 20-30 tons/day of edible food
- Existing food banks can't handle hot prepared meals

**Our Solution**:
- Nightly pickup from 15 fast food restaurants, pizza places, bakeries
- Deliver to 5 homeless shelters and sober living centers
- Automated tax documentation for restaurant partners
- Professional, reliable service (not volunteer-dependent)

**Innovation**:
- Mobile app for seamless restaurant partnerships
- IRS Form 8283 auto-generation for tax compliance
- Real-time tracking and impact reporting
